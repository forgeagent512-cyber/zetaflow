## 3. Demo Script — Exact kya bolna/dikhana hai (10-12 minute demo)

### Opening (30 seconds)
> "Aaj main aapko ek AI employee dikhaunga jo 24/7 aapke leads ko handle karta hai — website se ho ya WhatsApp se. Main live dikhata hoon kaise kaam karta hai."

### Live Demo (5 minutes)
1. Apna phone nikaalo, client ke saamne WhatsApp/chat widget par khud customer bankar type karo:
   > "Hi, mujhe Dubai Marina mein apartment chahiye"
2. AI ka jawab dikhne do (budget/area/timeline poochega)
3. 3-4 messages continue karo jaise real customer:
   - "2 bedroom chahiye"
   - "Budget around 3 million"
   - "Cash payment, 30 din mein lena hai"
4. AI ka final qualification message dikhao

**Yahan yeh line bolo:**
> "Dekho — AI ne khud budget, area, timeline, payment method sab pooch liya, bina kisi insaan ke."

### Backend Dikhao (3 minutes)
1. Google Sheet kholo, live dikhao ke naya lead automatically aa gaya
2. Lead score dikhao (e.g. 92/100 — HOT)
3. WhatsApp/Email alert dikhao jo tumhe mila

**Yahan yeh line bolo:**
> "Aapko sirf yeh HOT leads dekhne hain — cold/warm leads AI khud follow-up karega, aapka time waste nahi hoga."

### Value Pitch (2 minutes)
> "Abhi aapki team har inquiry manually reply karti hai — raat ko, weekend par bhi miss ho jati hongi. Yeh AI kabhi nahi sota. Aur jab bhi genuinely serious buyer aata hai, aapko turant pata chal jata hai."

### Close (1 minute)
> "Agar yeh pasand aaya, hum isko aapki actual company, aapki real listings, aapke WhatsApp number ke saath 7 din mein live kar dete hain. Setup cost [X], monthly [Y]. Chahoge to hum aaj hi start kar sakte hain."

## 4. Common Client Questions — Ready Answers

**Q: "Kya yeh galat jawab dega customer ko?"**

**Q: "Kitna time lagega humare liye setup karne mein?"**

**Q: "Agar customer Arabic mein baat kare?"**
